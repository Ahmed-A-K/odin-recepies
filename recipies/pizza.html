Thin-crust Pizza

Description

This delicious thin crust pizza dough recipe is so low maintenance, you can throw it together in just 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar & Water
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Knead the dough.
  4. Roll the dough into a circle.
